Campus Life

school library

Was built in 1980

The school library is named after one of the greatest exponents of his time, Mr. Abdul Ghaffar Madholvi . Books on every subject studied at the school level are kept here .For the benefits of students, books of various publishers are kept here for every subject, without exception. These are periodically renewed according to the latest syllabus and format .The library notice board provides every information and notice prior hand regarding every amendment in syllabus, or be it competitive tests, exams, Olympiads etc. One of the most important features of school library is that it is located a bit away from the main school building, which keeps it aloof from unnecessary disturbances. As a result, one finds it easier to concentrate and an atmosphere of ‘thirst for knowledge’ prevails. Apart from these, the school library has a regular subscription of newspapers and magazines providing every information of current affairs in varied field of interest.
